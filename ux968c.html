Persona: Pat Planner

To help understand the context of the software, here is one of our primary personas: Pat Planner.

Pat Planner

This convention is going to be the most inclusive and diverse one of its kind yet!

Tools and Technology

  • Expects to do all of planning on Desktop as opposed to Mobile
  • Online form/survey tool (Google Forms, Survey Monkey)
  • Word Processor (Microsoft Word, Google Docs)
  • Spreadsheet Program (Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets)
  • Email (Gmail, Office365 Outlook)
  • Calendar software (Google Calendar)

Demographics

  • Adult
  • Attends and volunteers for fan conventions
  • Reads and writes English fluently
  • Note: there is no particular age group, background, or gender associated with this persona.

Attributes

  • Conscious of social justice
  • Volunteering for conventions is a huge part of their life

Pat works a boring 9 to 5 job, and then comes home and spends the rest of the evening volunteering for conventions. Their friends and family are also involved, so "unrelated" gatherings often turn into impromptu meetings. Pat needs software that will help plan the convention program, keeping all the relevant information secure and in one place.

Pat has a few concerns about any software they are going to use for the convention. First and foremost, the privacy of participants is very important, especially as there are many famous authors. Also, many times in the past participants have found themselves assigned to program items that make them feel uncomfortable, or that they did not really want to be on, so following a consent-based model for assigning participants to program items is key.

Having a more diverse and inclusive convention is very important to Pat. Diverse and inclusive language is important to not alienate people, and diversity metrics are important to be able to track progress, or lack thereof. Any software needs to include both of those. All content should have a robust diversity review process, to help mitigate the impact of unconscious bias.
